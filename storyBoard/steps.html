The 5 Steps to Create a Storyboard

1. Define Your Idea and Write a Script

Before drawing, write the complete story with dialogue and scene descriptions.

2. Break the Script into Key Scenes

Identify the most important moments that need to be visualized.

3. Draw the Storyboard Frames

You don't need to be a professional artist. Use simple sketches with arrows and notes.

4. Add Notes and Technical Details

Include camera angles, movements, sounds, and transitions.

5. Review and Adjust

Share with your team, get feedback, and improve your storyboard.
